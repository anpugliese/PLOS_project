More than 50% of the world's population lives in urban areas

Globally, more people live in urban areas than in rural areas, with an estimate 55% of the world’s population residing in cities in 2018. By 2030, urban areas are projected to house 60 per cent of people globally and one in every three people will live in cities with at least half a million inhabitants.

Growth in the urban population is driven by overall population increase and by the upward shift in the percentage living in urban areas. Together, these two factors are projected to add 2.5 billion to the world’s urban population by 2050, with almost 90 % of this growth happening in Asia and Africa.
